Admission Procedure 2025-26

Explore the guided pathway to join SBS Academy. We have designed a transparent and student-friendly admission process that helps families make informed decisions with confidence.

How Admissions Work at SBS Academy

Every application is reviewed holistically. We value curiosity, effort, and a supportive home environment just as much as prior academic performance. The procedure below outlines what to expect from enquiry to enrolment.

Key Highlights

  • Admissions open for Nursery to Class IX and Class XI (Science & Commerce).
  • Limited seats ensure personal attention and optimal teacher-student ratio.
  • Scholarships offered on merit-cum-means basis for Classes VI and above.
  • Bridge courses support transitions for new students joining higher grades.

Important Dates (Tentative)

  • Registration Window: 15 December 2024 – 15 February 2025.
  • Assessment Days: Every alternate Saturday starting 4 January 2025.
  • Interaction with Principal: 3 working days post assessment.
  • Offer & Fee Submission: Within 7 days from selection intimation.

Step-by-Step Admission Journey

Follow the streamlined six-step journey that keeps families informed at every milestone. Our admission counsellors remain available throughout to answer questions.

1

Campus Enquiry

Visit the admission desk or call 8977656553 to schedule a counselling slot. Download the digital prospectus and share your ward's latest academic record for reference.

2

Registration Form

Complete the registration form available online or at the campus. Submit it with a non-refundable registration fee of INR 500 along with the required documents.

3

Assessment & Interaction

Nursery to Class II participate in a playful readiness interaction. Class III onwards attempt a 60-minute baseline assessment in English, Mathematics, and Environmental Studies/Science.

4

Principal Meeting

Parents and students meet the Principal to discuss learning goals, school philosophy, and bridge plans. Seats are offered based on readiness, seat availability, and diversity goals.

5

Offer & Confirmation

Selected families receive an offer letter and fee schedule via email and SMS. Confirm the seat within seven working days by paying the admission fee.

6

Orientation & Onboarding

Attend the Parent Orientation Session, collect the student handbook, and sign up for the Smart ID card and transport services if required.

Eligibility & Entry Points

We strive to offer age-appropriate learning experiences. Applications will be considered for below entry points, subject to seat availability.

Pre-Primary Nursery (3+ years), LKG (4+ years), UKG (5+ years).
Primary Classes I – V: Age +2 benchmark with prior school record.
Middle & Secondary Classes VI – IX: Transfer Certificate and diagnostic assessment mandatory.
Senior Secondary Class XI: Minimum 60% in Class X Board exams with subject-specific prerequisite.
Please note that age will be calculated as on 31 March 2025 in accordance with CBSE norms. Exceptional cases are considered by the Principal on recommendation of the academic council.

Documents Checklist

Keep clear photocopies and originals ready for verification. Digital scans may be emailed to admissions@sbsacademy.edu for faster processing.

Mandatory for All
  • Birth Certificate issued by Municipal Authority.
  • Aadhar Card of student and both parents.
  • Four recent passport size photographs of the student.
  • Parent ID proof and residential proof.
Grade-Specific
  • Classes I – IX: Previous school report card and Transfer Certificate.
  • Class XI: Class X Board Marksheet, Migration Certificate (if applicable).
  • Scholarship cases: Income certificate and previous accolades.
Optional (If Applicable)
  • Medical reports for special accommodations.
  • Sibling Proof (ID card or fee receipt) for concession requests.
  • Transport requirement form with pick-up location details.

Fee Overview

Detailed class-wise fee structure is available on the Fee Structure page. Below is a quick reference to help you plan ahead.

At the Time of Admission

  • Admission Fee (one-time, non-refundable).
  • Security Deposit (refundable at the time of exit).
  • First Quarter Tuition Fee.
  • Annual Resource & Lab Charges.

Payment Modes

  • Online: Net banking, UPI, and debit/credit cards.
  • Offline: Cheque or Demand Draft in favor of "Sardar Bhagat Singh Academy".
  • Installments: Quarterly payment schedule with reminders.
  • Sibling Benefit: 10% tuition fee waiver on the younger sibling.
Transport, books, and uniforms are charged separately based on actuals. Scholarships and concessions are adjusted in the second fee invoice upon submission of relevant documents.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a boarding facility?

SBS Academy is a day school. We provide transport services across major neighborhoods of Gorakhpur.

Are mid-session admissions allowed?

Mid-session admissions are considered only if seats open up due to transfers. Submit an enquiry to be on the priority waitlist.

How are scholarships awarded?

Scholarships are based on written assessment scores, personal interview, and verification of income documents. Continuation depends on annual performance.

What support is provided for new students?

We run a two-week bridge programme, peer buddy system, and regular counselling check-ins to ensure smooth integration into the SBS learning culture.
