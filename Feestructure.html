Payment Schedule

Parents can choose to pay the annual fee in one installment or opt for the quarterly payment plan for convenience.

Option 1: Annual Payment

  • Pay the entire annual fee at the beginning of the academic session
  • 5% discount on total annual fee (excluding admission fee)
  • Due date: Before 15th April
  • Most economical option

Option 2: Quarterly Installments

  • Pay fees in 4 equal quarterly installments

Installment Schedule:

  • 1st Installment: Before 15th April (30%)
  • 2nd Installment: Before 15th July (25%)
  • 3rd Installment: Before 15th October (25%)
  • 4th Installment: Before 15th January (20%)

Scholarships & Financial Aid

SBS Academy is committed to making quality education accessible. We offer various scholarships and financial assistance programs for deserving students.

Merit-Based Scholarships

  • Top 3 students in each class: 25% fee waiver
  • Students scoring 95%+ in board exams: 30% fee waiver
  • Annual scholarship exam for Classes VI-X: Up to 50% waiver
  • Renewable based on academic performance

Need-Based Financial Aid

  • Available for economically weaker sections
  • Requires income certificate and application
  • Up to 40% fee concession based on need
  • Annual review and renewal process

Application Process: Scholarship and financial aid applications are accepted at the beginning of each academic year. Please contact the school administration office for detailed information and application forms.

Refund Policy

Our refund policy is designed to be fair and transparent, ensuring clarity for all parents.

Refund Guidelines

  • Admission Fee: Non-refundable under any circumstances
  • Tuition Fee: Refundable on pro-rata basis if withdrawal is made within 30 days of admission
  • After 30 Days: No refund of tuition fee will be provided
  • Development Fee: Non-refundable after payment
  • Other Charges: Refundable only if services are not availed
  • All refund requests must be submitted in writing to the Principal
  • Refunds will be processed within 30-45 working days

Important Notes

  • All fees are subject to annual revision as per school policy
  • Fee structure may vary for transfer students mid-session
  • Transportation fee (if applicable) is charged separately
  • Uniform and books are to be purchased separately as per school requirements
  • Examination fees for board exams (Classes X & XII) are charged separately
  • Parents are required to submit fee receipts for all transactions
  • Fee defaulters may result in suspension of student from classes
  • For any fee-related queries, please contact the accounts office
