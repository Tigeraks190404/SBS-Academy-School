School Overview

SBS Academy, established in 2019, stands as a premier educational institution in Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh. Affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), we have been committed to providing quality education that shapes young minds into future leaders.

Our school was founded with a vision to create an educational environment that combines academic excellence with character building. Over the years, we have grown from a small institution to a well-established school, serving hundreds of students from diverse backgrounds.

Located in the heart of Gorakhpur, our campus spans across acres of land, providing ample space for learning, recreation, and growth. We take pride in our state-of-the-art infrastructure that supports both academic and extracurricular activities.

At SBS Academy, we believe that education is a partnership between students, parents, and teachers. We work collaboratively to ensure that every student receives the attention, support, and opportunities they need to succeed academically and personally.

Our Faculty

Our teaching staff comprises highly qualified and experienced educators who are passionate about teaching and committed to student success. All our teachers hold appropriate degrees and certifications, with many having advanced qualifications in their respective subjects.

Our faculty members are selected not just for their academic qualifications, but also for their dedication, empathy, and ability to connect with students. They regularly participate in professional development programs and workshops to stay updated with the latest teaching methodologies and educational trends.

We maintain a healthy student-teacher ratio to ensure that each student receives individual attention. Our teachers go beyond the classroom to mentor students, guide them in their academic pursuits, and help them navigate challenges both inside and outside school.

Continuous Professional Development

We believe in investing in our teachers' growth. Regular training sessions, workshops, and seminars are conducted to help our faculty stay abreast of modern teaching techniques and educational innovations.

Our teachers are encouraged to pursue further studies, attend conferences, and collaborate with educators from other institutions to share best practices and improve teaching effectiveness.

Our Commitment

At SBS Academy, we are committed to providing a nurturing, inclusive, and academically rigorous environment where students can discover their passions, develop their talents, and prepare for success in higher education and beyond. We take pride in our role in shaping the leaders of tomorrow.
